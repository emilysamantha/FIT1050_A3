Redwood Gardens Chinese restaurant has been established since 1995 and has a seating capacity of up to 125 people. The restaurant has sweeping views overlooking a man made lake and is situated in the center of an industrial area. Our dishes incorporate a variety of Asian cuisines. We cater to all sorts of functions and banquets can be set up to meet individual needs.
